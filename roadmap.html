Roadmap

The Idea

A need for a quantum resistant solution for blockchain technology was identified. A major vulnerability and unacceptable level of risk tolerance by current developers was evident in the crypto-asset space. Despite growing evidence of the need for preparation from quantum cryptographers and intelligence agencies to the investment in quantum computing by financial institutions, governments and billion dollar funds.

August 2016

Whitepaper Release

After discussion with developers, post-quantum cryptographers and research into existing roadmap of blockchain technology, the whitepaper was written to provide an overview of quantum resistant methodologies. The Winternitz OTS+ and Extended Merkle Signature Scheme were identified as the optimal solutions. This investigation also revealed the necessity of QRL to stand alone as a network with interoperability in the later pipeline.

November 2016

Ledger Design

The basic network design with the integration of a basic POS algorithm and quantum resistant signatures.

February 2017

Alpha Block Explorer

Design and Launch of the first iteration of the block explorer.

February 2017

Alpha Testnet

Launching of the Alpha TestNet with nodes run on MacOs, Linux, Raspberry Pi and Windows.

March 2017

Design of Proof of Stake Protocol

The design of the basic protocol to support Proof of Stake provides incentive to nodes to invest their tokens as working assets. This design would provide a level of liquidity to ease flow of assets within the network while rewarding long term investment.

April 2017

Pre-sale

Pre-sale of the tokens to fund 3 years of development in the pursuit of hosting over a billion dollars of digitial assets. The target is 4 Million, with a hard cap at 5 million. This will fund the end-end quantum resistant encrypted communication layer, development of the framework for digital provenance (proof of ownership), multi factor identification and governance (voting and consensus development). It will fund opportunities for developing new protocols (smart contracts), use-cases, education, partnership and other activities to bolster the networks value. It will also help fund bounties that strengthen the network value.

May 2017

Public TestNet

Opportunity for developers to start accessing and building on the system. Identification of bugs that prohibit scaling.

May 2017

Project Ephemeral Framework

End-End Quantum Resistant Encrypted Communitcation. This functionality of the blockchain using Lattice Based Cryptography, such as New Hope, will provide a foundation for secure communication and transactions within the network.

June 2017

Main Net Launch

The launch of the Public Network and distrbution of the presold tokens.

August 2017

PQ Digital Provenance

The development of a framework, API and initial partnerships to create proof of ownership protocol that can be used for digital assets and associated with physical objects just in time for Christmas

December 2017

PQ Digital Identification

An ultra secure multifactor identification framework to support individuals reclaiming their identity.

February 2018

End Versioning Programming

A rebuild of the network using alternative approaches to reduce vulnerabilities in an individual design.

February 2018

PQ Governance

The development of a framework to support polling, elections and developing consensus. The consensus development framework will utilize neural networking to group common ideas and suggestions. A reputation system and interior economy will also provide a democratized platform for dialogue and decisionmaking.

July 2018
